JARVIS-Your Digital Companion

Jarvis is an advanced AI-powered virtual assistant designed to simplify your daily tasks and provide intelligent assistance through natural language commands. Built with cutting-edge Python technologies, Jarvis combines multiple functionalities into one seamless experience.

Key Features:

  • Voice Interaction - Natural text-to-speech responses with customizable voice settings
  • Web Automation - Open websites and search content with voice commands
  • Information Mastery - Get Wikipedia summaries, news headlines, and weather reports
  • Productivity Tools - Check time, dates (including Hijri calendar)
  • Entertainment - Enjoy AI-generated jokes and creative image generation
  • AI Integration - Powered by DeepSeek AI for intelligent question answering

Technical Highlights:

  • Built with Python using PyTTSx3 for voice synthesis
  • Integrates with OpenRouter AI, Wikipedia API, and NewsAPI
  • Weather data from OpenWeatherMap
  • Web automation through Selenium
  • Hijri-Gregorian date conversion capabilities

This assistant was created by khalid as a comprehensive solution blending artificial intelligence with practical daily utilities. Jarvis represents the next generation of personal assistants - adaptable, intelligent, and always ready to help.
